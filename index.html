Why Cyber Security Upskill?

Cybersecurity professionals who can protect and defend an organization's network are in high demand right now. A projected job growth showed that there will be 3.5 million unfilled cybersecurity jobs globally by 2021. The program was conceived and aimed at providing participants with on-the-job IT experience and certification prior to the engagement of their potential employers. It also provides the participants the platform to connect with peers, professionals, and their potential employers irrespective of their geographical location or genders.
